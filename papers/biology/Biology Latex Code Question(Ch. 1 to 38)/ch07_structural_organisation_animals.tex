\begin{enumerate}[label=Q\arabic*.]

\item Which type of epithelial tissue lines the alveoli of lungs to facilitate gas exchange?
\\(A) Stratified squamous epithelium
\\(B) Simple squamous epithelium
\\(C) Columnar epithelium
\\(D) Cuboidal epithelium

\item The tissue that connects bone to bone is:
\\(A) Tendon
\\(B) Ligament
\\(C) Cartilage
\\(D) Fascia

\item Which type of muscle tissue is involuntary and striated?
\\(A) Skeletal muscle
\\(B) Smooth muscle
\\(C) Cardiac muscle
\\(D) Both skeletal and cardiac

\item Mast cells are found in which type of connective tissue and are involved in:
\\(A) Dense connective tissue; structural support
\\(B) Loose connective tissue; releasing histamine in immune responses
\\(C) Cartilage; producing collagen
\\(D) Blood; oxygen transport

\item In the earthworm, the body wall from outside to inside is arranged as:
\\(A) Cuticle $\rightarrow$ Epidermis $\rightarrow$ Circular muscle $\rightarrow$ Longitudinal muscle $\rightarrow$ Coelomic epithelium
\\(B) Epidermis $\rightarrow$ Cuticle $\rightarrow$ Longitudinal muscle $\rightarrow$ Circular muscle
\\(C) Cuticle $\rightarrow$ Longitudinal muscle $\rightarrow$ Circular muscle $\rightarrow$ Epidermis
\\(D) Peritoneum $\rightarrow$ Circular muscle $\rightarrow$ Longitudinal muscle $\rightarrow$ Cuticle

\item The gizzard (proventriculus) in earthworm functions to:
\\(A) Absorb digested nutrients
\\(B) Grind food particles
\\(C) Secrete digestive enzymes
\\(D) Produce mucus for lubrication

\item Assertion: In cockroach, blood (haemolymph) does not transport oxygen.\\
Reason: Cockroach has an open circulatory system and respires through a tracheal system.
\\(A) Both true, Reason is correct explanation
\\(B) Both true, Reason is not correct explanation
\\(C) Assertion true, Reason false
\\(D) Assertion false, Reason true

\item Which organ in the cockroach is responsible for excretion of nitrogenous wastes?
\\(A) Nephridia
\\(B) Flame cells
\\(C) Malpighian tubules
\\(D) Kidneys

\item In frogs, the skin performs which additional physiological function beyond protection?
\\(A) Photosynthesis
\\(B) Cutaneous respiration (gas exchange)
\\(C) Thermoregulation by sweating
\\(D) Excretion of uric acid

\item Which type of connective tissue has a fluid matrix and includes plasma, RBCs, WBCs, and platelets?
\\(A) Areolar tissue
\\(B) Adipose tissue
\\(C) Blood
\\(D) Lymph

\item The neuroglia cells in nervous tissue function as:
\\(A) Transmitting nerve impulses
\\(B) Providing structural and metabolic support to neurons
\\(C) Producing myelin sheath only
\\(D) Secreting neurotransmitters

\item In the earthworm, the typhlosole is a structure that:
\\(A) Pumps blood through the body
\\(B) Increases absorptive surface area of the intestine
\\(C) Grinds food in the gizzard
\\(D) Stores reproductive cells

\item Hyaline cartilage, fibrous cartilage, and elastic cartilage differ primarily in:
\\(A) Cell type
\\(B) Type and amount of fibres in the matrix
\\(C) Location only
\\(D) Mode of nutrient supply

\item Match the following animal tissues with their locations:\\
1. Simple cuboidal epithelium $\rightarrow$ (a) Kidney tubules\\
2. Pseudostratified epithelium $\rightarrow$ (b) Trachea\\
3. Transitional epithelium $\rightarrow$ (c) Urinary bladder\\
4. Stratified squamous $\rightarrow$ (d) Skin
\\(A) 1-a, 2-b, 3-c, 4-d
\\(B) 1-b, 2-a, 3-d, 4-c
\\(C) 1-c, 2-d, 3-a, 4-b
\\(D) 1-d, 2-b, 3-a, 4-c

\item The gonads (testes/ovaries) in cockroach are located in which abdominal segments?
\\(A) 1st to 3rd abdominal segments
\\(B) 4th and 5th abdominal segments (testes); 2nd to 6th (ovaries)
\\(C) 7th to 9th abdominal segments
\\(D) Throughout all abdominal segments

\end{enumerate}
