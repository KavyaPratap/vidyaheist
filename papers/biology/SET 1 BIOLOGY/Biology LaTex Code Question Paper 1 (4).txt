\section*{Biology Set 1 - Paper 4 (Questions)}

\begin{enumerate}
\item [Diversity] Which phylum includes organisms with a notochord at some stage?  
(A) Arthropoda (B) Mollusca (C) Chordata (D) Annelida

\item [Cell Biology] Which organelle is responsible for autophagy?  
(A) Lysosome (B) Golgi (C) Ribosome (D) Peroxisome

\item [Genetics] Which type of cross is used to study two traits simultaneously?  
(A) Monohybrid (B) Dihybrid (C) Test cross (D) Back cross

\item [Plant Physiology] Which process occurs in guard cells to open stomata?  
(A) Loss of water (B) Gain of K\(^+\) ions (C) Photosynthesis (D) Respiration

\item [Human Physiology] Which part of the nephron is impermeable to water?  
(A) Descending loop of Henle (B) Ascending loop of Henle (C) Collecting duct (D) Proximal tubule

\item [Ecology] Which interaction describes one species benefiting while the other is unaffected?  
(A) Mutualism (B) Commensalism (C) Parasitism (D) Competition

\item [Cell Biology] Which structure organizes spindle fibers during mitosis in animal cells?  
(A) Centriole (B) Nucleolus (C) Ribosome (D) Golgi

\item [Genetics] Which condition is caused by trisomy of chromosome 21?  
(A) Turner syndrome (B) Down syndrome (C) Klinefelter syndrome (D) Edwards syndrome

\item [Plant Physiology] Which pigment is essential for photosystem II?  
(A) Chlorophyll a (B) Chlorophyll b (C) Carotene (D) Xanthophyll

\item [Human Physiology] Which hormone increases blood calcium levels?  
(A) Calcitonin (B) Parathyroid hormone (C) Insulin (D) Thyroxine

\item [Diversity] Which group of plants produces naked seeds?  
(A) Angiosperms (B) Gymnosperms (C) Bryophytes (D) Pteridophytes

\item [Cell Biology] Which enzyme relieves supercoiling during DNA replication?  
(A) Ligase (B) Helicase (C) Topoisomerase (D) Polymerase

\item [Ecology] Which term describes the gradual change in species composition over time?  
(A) Succession (B) Evolution (C) Speciation (D) Migration

\item [Human Physiology] Which part of the brain regulates voluntary movement?  
(A) Cerebrum (B) Cerebellum (C) Medulla (D) Hypothalamus

\item [Biotechnology] Which technique is used to detect specific proteins?  
(A) PCR (B) ELISA (C) Western blot (D) Gel electrophoresis
\end{enumerate}
