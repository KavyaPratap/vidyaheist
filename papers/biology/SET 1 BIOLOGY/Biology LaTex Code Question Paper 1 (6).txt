\section*{Biology Set 1 - Paper 6 (Questions)}

\begin{enumerate}
\item [Diversity] Which group of animals is diploblastic?  
(A) Porifera (B) Cnidaria (C) Annelida (D) Arthropoda

\item [Cell Biology] Which organelle is responsible for protein glycosylation?  
(A) Ribosome (B) Golgi apparatus (C) Lysosome (D) Mitochondrion

\item [Genetics] Which type of mutation involves insertion or deletion of nucleotides not in multiples of three?  
(A) Missense (B) Nonsense (C) Frameshift (D) Silent

\item [Plant Physiology] Which process explains movement of water across a semipermeable membrane?  
(A) Diffusion (B) Osmosis (C) Active transport (D) Facilitated diffusion

\item [Human Physiology] Which hormone regulates circadian rhythm?  
(A) Cortisol (B) Melatonin (C) Insulin (D) Thyroxine

\item [Ecology] Which term describes the maximum population size an environment can sustain?  
(A) Biotic potential (B) Carrying capacity (C) Population density (D) Limiting factor

\item [Cell Biology] Which structure is responsible for photosynthesis in cyanobacteria?  
(A) Chloroplast (B) Thylakoid membranes (C) Ribosome (D) Nucleoid

\item [Genetics] Which principle states that allele frequencies remain constant in absence of evolutionary forces?  
(A) Law of Segregation (B) Hardy-Weinberg equilibrium (C) Law of Dominance (D) Genetic drift

\item [Plant Physiology] Which mineral is essential for nitrogen fixation in legumes?  
(A) Iron (B) Molybdenum (C) Magnesium (D) Potassium

\item [Human Physiology] Which structure prevents food from entering the trachea?  
(A) Epiglottis (B) Larynx (C) Pharynx (D) Glottis

\item [Diversity] Which group of plants is non-vascular?  
(A) Bryophytes (B) Pteridophytes (C) Gymnosperms (D) Angiosperms

\item [Cell Biology] Which enzyme synthesizes DNA from RNA template?  
(A) DNA polymerase (B) Reverse transcriptase (C) Ligase (D) Primase

\item [Ecology] Which interaction describes both species being harmed?  
(A) Mutualism (B) Commensalism (C) Competition (D) Parasitism

\item [Human Physiology] Which part of the eye regulates amount of light entering?  
(A) Lens (B) Retina (C) Iris (D) Cornea

\item [Biotechnology] Which technique is used to amplify RNA sequences?  
(A) PCR (B) RT-PCR (C) ELISA (D) Western blot
\end{enumerate}
