\section*{Biology Set 1 - Paper 1 (Questions)}

\begin{enumerate}
\item [Diversity] Which kingdom includes organisms with chitinous cell walls?  
(A) Plantae (B) Fungi (C) Protista (D) Monera

\item [Cell Biology] Which organelle is the site of oxidative phosphorylation?  
(A) Ribosome (B) Mitochondrion (C) Golgi (D) Chloroplast

\item [Genetics] In a dihybrid cross AaBb × AaBb, what is the expected phenotypic ratio?  
(A) 9:3:3:1 (B) 3:1 (C) 1:2:1 (D) 1:1

\item [Plant Physiology] Which hormone promotes seed dormancy?  
(A) Auxin (B) ABA (C) Gibberellin (D) Cytokinin

\item [Human Physiology] Which part of the nephron is responsible for maximum water reabsorption?  
(A) Collecting duct (B) Loop of Henle (C) Proximal tubule (D) Distal tubule

\item [Ecology] Which biome has permafrost?  
(A) Desert (B) Tundra (C) Grassland (D) Rainforest

\item [Cell Biology] Which phase of mitosis involves chromosome alignment at the equator?  
(A) Prophase (B) Metaphase (C) Anaphase (D) Telophase

\item [Genetics] Which phenomenon explains non-Mendelian inheritance of mitochondrial genes?  
(A) Epistasis (B) Cytoplasmic inheritance (C) Codominance (D) Linkage

\item [Plant Physiology] Which pigment detects red and far-red light?  
(A) Chlorophyll (B) Phytochrome (C) Carotene (D) Xanthophyll

\item [Human Physiology] Which blood vessel carries oxygenated blood from lungs to heart?  
(A) Pulmonary artery (B) Pulmonary vein (C) Aorta (D) Vena cava

\item [Diversity] Which phylum has jointed appendages?  
(A) Mollusca (B) Arthropoda (C) Annelida (D) Echinodermata

\item [Cell Biology] Which enzyme unwinds DNA during replication?  
(A) Ligase (B) Helicase (C) Polymerase (D) Topoisomerase

\item [Ecology] What is the term for species first colonizing bare rock?  
(A) Keystone species (B) Pioneer species (C) Endemic species (D) Indicator species

\item [Human Physiology] Which part of the brain regulates thermoregulation?  
(A) Cerebrum (B) Cerebellum (C) Hypothalamus (D) Medulla

\item [Biotechnology] Which technique is used to amplify DNA?  
(A) PCR (B) ELISA (C) Gel electrophoresis (D) Western blot
\end{enumerate}
