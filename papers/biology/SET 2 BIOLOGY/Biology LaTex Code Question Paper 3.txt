\section*{Biology Set 2 - Paper 3 (Questions)}

\begin{enumerate}
\item [Diversity] Which feature distinguishes *Echinodermata* larvae from adults?  
(A) Bilateral symmetry in larvae, radial in adults  
(B) Radial symmetry in both stages  
(C) Segmentation in larvae, none in adults  
(D) Presence of notochord in larvae

\item [Cell Biology] In eukaryotic cells, which checkpoint ensures DNA is fully replicated before mitosis?  
(A) G1 checkpoint  
(B) G2/M checkpoint  
(C) Metaphase checkpoint  
(D) Cytokinesis checkpoint

\item [Genetics] A heterozygous individual for two linked genes (AaBb) produces gametes in which ratio if recombination frequency is 20%?  
(A) 40% AB, 40% ab, 10% Ab, 10% aB  
(B) 25% each gamete type  
(C) 50% parental, 50% recombinant  
(D) 80% parental, 20% recombinant

(A) Active transport of K\(^+\) into guard cells  
(B) Passive diffusion of CO\(_2\)  
(C) ABA accumulation

\item [Human Physiology] Which part of the nephron establishes the osmotic gradient essential for water reabsorption?  
(A) Proximal tubule  
(B) Loop of Henle  
(C) Distal tubule  
(D) Collecting duct

\item [Ecology] In a food chain, if 1000 J of energy is available at producer level, how much energy typically reaches tertiary consumers?  
(A) 100 J  
(B) 10 J  
(C) 1 J

\item [Cell Biology] Which enzyme is responsible for proofreading during DNA replication in prokaryotes?  
(A) DNA polymerase I  
(B) DNA polymerase III  
(C) Ligase  
(D) Helicase

\item [Genetics] Which phenomenon explains inheritance of traits like human skin color?  
(A) Codominance  
(B) Incomplete dominance  
(C) Polygenic inheritance  
(D) Epistasis

\item [Plant Physiology] Which mineral deficiency leads to chlorosis in young leaves due to its immobility?  
(A) Calcium  
(B) Iron  
(C) Magnesium  
(D) Nitrogen

\item [Human Physiology] Which structure of the brain integrates autonomic functions like respiration and heart rate?  
(A) Cerebrum  
(B) Cerebellum  
(C) Medulla oblongata

\item [Diversity] Which group of plants shows alternation of generations with dominant sporophyte?  
(A) Bryophytes  
(B) Pteridophytes  
(C) Gymnosperms  
(D) Angiosperms

\item [Cell Biology] Which organelle is involved in photorespiration in plants along with mitochondria and chloroplasts?  
(A) Peroxisome  
(B) Lysosome  
(C) Golgi  
(D) Vacuole

\item [Ecology] Which term describes the gradual replacement of one community by another in an ecosystem?  
(A) Succession  
(B) Speciation  
(C) Evolution  
(D) Migration

\item [Human Physiology] Which hormone stimulates reabsorption of sodium ions in kidney tubules?  
(A) Insulin  
(B) Aldosterone  
(C) Cortisol  
(D) ADH

\item [Biotechnology] Which technique uses fluorescently labeled dideoxynucleotides to terminate DNA synthesis?  
(A) PCR  
(B) Sanger sequencing  
(C) Gel electrophoresis  
(D) ELISA
