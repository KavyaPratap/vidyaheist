\section*{Biology Set 2 - Paper 1 (Questions)}

\begin{enumerate}
\item [Diversity] Which group of animals shows pentamerous radial symmetry in adults?  
(A) Mollusca (B) Arthropoda (C) Echinodermata (D) Annelida

\item [Cell Biology] Which organelle is responsible for detoxification of drugs in liver cells?  
(A) Rough ER (B) Smooth ER (C) Golgi (D) Lysosome

\item [Genetics] Which genetic principle explains the inheritance of traits located on the same chromosome?  
(A) Independent assortment (B) Linkage (C) Codominance (D) Epistasis

\item [Plant Physiology] Which hormone breaks seed dormancy and promotes germination?  
(A) Auxin (B) Gibberellin (C) ABA (D) Cytokinin

\item [Human Physiology] Which structure in the nephron is responsible for selective reabsorption of glucose?  
(A) Proximal tubule (B) Loop of Henle (C) Distal tubule (D) Collecting duct

\item [Ecology] Which type of succession occurs after forest fire?  
(A) Primary succession (B) Secondary succession (C) Retrogressive succession (D) Climax succession

\item [Cell Biology] Which enzyme seals nicks in DNA backbone during replication?  
(A) Helicase (B) Ligase (C) Polymerase (D) Primase

\item [Genetics] Which condition is caused by an extra X chromosome in males (XXY)?  
(A) Turner syndrome (B) Down syndrome (C) Klinefelter syndrome (D) Edwards syndrome

\item [Plant Physiology] Which tissue transports water in plants?  
(A) Phloem (B) Xylem (C) Cambium (D) Epidermis

\item [Human Physiology] Which hormone increases heart rate and blood pressure during stress?  
(A) Insulin (B) Adrenaline (C) Cortisol (D) Thyroxine

\item [Diversity] Which kingdom includes prokaryotic organisms?  
(A) Protista (B) Monera (C) Fungi (D) Plantae

\item [Cell Biology] Which structure is responsible for ATP synthesis in mitochondria?  
(A) Cristae (B) Matrix (C) Outer membrane (D) Ribosome

\item [Ecology] Which factor is abiotic?  
(A) Bacteria (B) Temperature (C) Fungi (D) Plants

\item [Human Physiology] Which blood component transports oxygen?  
(A) Plasma (B) RBC (C) WBC (D) Platelets

\item [Biotechnology] Which technique is used to amplify RNA after conversion to cDNA?  
(A) PCR (B) RT-PCR (C) ELISA (D) Western blot
\end{enumerate}
