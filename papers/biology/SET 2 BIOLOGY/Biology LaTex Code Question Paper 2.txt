\section*{Biology Set 2 - Paper 2 (Questions)}

\begin{enumerate}
\item [Diversity] Which phylum includes organisms with a pseudocoelom?  
(A) Annelida (B) Nematoda (C) Arthropoda (D) Mollusca

\item [Cell Biology] Which organelle is responsible for protein folding quality control?  
(A) Rough ER (B) Golgi apparatus (C) Lysosome (D) Ribosome

\item [Genetics] Which genetic disorder is caused by a single base substitution in the hemoglobin gene?  
(A) Thalassemia (B) Sickle cell anemia (C) Hemophilia (D) Down syndrome

\item [Plant Physiology] Which process explains guttation in plants?  
(A) Root pressure (B) Transpiration (C) Diffusion (D) Osmosis

\item [Human Physiology] Which hormone stimulates uterine contractions during childbirth?  
(A) Progesterone (B) Oxytocin (C) Estrogen (D) Prolactin

\item [Ecology] Which type of species has a disproportionately large effect on ecosystem structure?  
(A) Indicator species (B) Keystone species (C) Endemic species (D) Exotic species

\item [Cell Biology] Which enzyme removes RNA primers during DNA replication in prokaryotes?  
(A) DNA polymerase I (B) DNA polymerase III (C) Ligase (D) Helicase

\item [Genetics] Which law states that alleles segregate during gamete formation?  
(A) Law of Segregation (B) Law of Independent Assortment (C) Hardy-Weinberg principle (D) Genetic drift

\item [Plant Physiology] Which mineral is essential for synthesis of nucleic acids?  
(A) Nitrogen (B) Phosphorus (C) Magnesium (D) Potassium

\item [Human Physiology] Which structure in the heart prevents backflow of blood into atria?  
(A) Semilunar valves (B) AV valves (C) Pulmonary valve (D) Aortic valve

\item [Diversity] Which group of plants produces spores but lacks seeds?  
(A) Bryophytes (B) Pteridophytes (C) Gymnosperms (D) Angiosperms

\item [Cell Biology] Which structure is responsible for chromosome movement during cell division?  
(A) Spindle fibers (B) Centrioles (C) Nucleolus (D) Ribosomes

\item [Ecology] Which term describes the feeding position of an organism in a food chain?  
(A) Niche (B) Trophic level (C) Habitat (D) Succession

\item [Human Physiology] Which hormone regulates basal metabolic rate?  
(A) Insulin (B) Thyroxine (C) Cortisol (D) Adrenaline

\item [Biotechnology] Which technique is used to determine nucleotide sequence of DNA?  
(A) PCR (B) DNA sequencing (C) Gel electrophoresis (D) ELISA
\end{enumerate}
