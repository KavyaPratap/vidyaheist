\section*{Question Paper}

\subsection*{Section A: Multi-Concept (5 Questions)}
\begin{enumerate}[label=Q\arabic*.]
\item A transgenic \textit{Triticum aestivum} (wheat) plant expresses a gene for protein enrichment. Which NCERT concept does this illustrate?  
(A) Biofortification  
(B) Bt cotton for pest resistance  
(C) Somatic hybridization  
(D) Micropropagation

\item A mutation in the gene coding for chloride ion channels leads to cystic fibrosis. Which combined physiological effect is observed?  
(A) Thick mucus secretion + impaired gas exchange  
(B) Enhanced immunity + anemia  
(C) Increased bone density + reduced calcium absorption  
(D) Excessive sweating + hyperthyroidism

\item In a dihybrid cross AaBb × AaBb, what fraction of progeny will show both recessive traits according to Mendel’s law?  
(A) 1/16  
(B) 3/16  
(C) 4/16  
(D) 9/16

\item A patient with parathyroid gland removal shows low calcium levels. Which NCERT concept explains this?  
(A) Hypoparathyroidism leading to tetany  
(B) Hyperthyroidism  
(C) Adrenal insufficiency  
(D) Diabetes mellitus

\item In a population of \textit{Drosophila}, allele frequency of A = 0.7 and a = 0.3. Under Hardy–Weinberg equilibrium, what is expected frequency of heterozygotes?  
(A) 0.21  
(B) 0.42  
(C) 0.49  
(D) 0.09
\end{enumerate}

\subsection*{Section B: Data/Experimental (5 Questions)}

(A) Lag phase  
(B) Exponential phase

\item A restriction digestion of plasmid yields fragments of 6 kb and 2 kb. Which NCERT technique confirms fragment sizes?  
(A) SDS-PAGE  
(B) Agarose gel electrophoresis  
(C) ELISA  
(D) Western blot

\item Photosynthetic rate in isolated chloroplasts is measured under red light vs. white light. Which observation is correct?  
(A) Red light excites only PSI  
(B) Red light excites only PSII  
(C) White light excites both PSI and PSII  
(D) Red light inhibits Calvin cycle
\end{enumerate}

\subsection*{Section C: Assertion-Reason / Matching (5 Questions)}
\begin{enumerate}[label=Q\arabic*.]
\setcounter{enumi}{10}
\item Assertion (A): DNA polymerase cannot initiate replication without a primer.  
Reason (R): RNA primers provide free 3'-OH ends for elongation.  
(A) Both A and R true, R correct explanation  
(B) Both A and R true, R not explanation  
(C) A true, R false  
(D) A false, R true

\item Assertion (A): ADH secretion increases during dehydration.  
Reason (R): ADH promotes water reabsorption in collecting ducts.  
(A) Both A and R true, R correct explanation  
(B) Both A and R true, R not explanation  
(C) A true, R false  
(D) A false, R true

\item Match the following (NCERT based):  
1. PCR $\rightarrow$ (a) DNA amplification  
2. ELISA $\rightarrow$ (b) Antigen-antibody detection  
3. Western blot $\rightarrow$ (c) Protein identification  
4. DNA microarray $\rightarrow$ (d) Gene expression profiling  

Correct match:  
(A) 1-a, 2-b, 3-c, 4-d  
(B) 1-b, 2-a, 3-d, 4-c  
(C) 1-c, 2-d, 3-a, 4-b  
(D) 1-a, 2-c, 3-b, 4-d

\item Assertion (A): Density-independent factors regulate population growth.  
Reason (R): Floods affect populations regardless of density.  
(A) Both A and R true, R correct explanation  
(B) Both A and R true, R not explanation  
(C) A true, R false  
(D) A false, R true

\item Assertion (A): In lac operon, lactose acts as inducer.  
Reason (R): Lactose binds repressor, preventing operator binding.  
(A) Both A and R true, R correct explanation  
(B) Both A and R true, R not explanation  
(C) A true, R false  
(D) A false, R true
\end{enumerate}
