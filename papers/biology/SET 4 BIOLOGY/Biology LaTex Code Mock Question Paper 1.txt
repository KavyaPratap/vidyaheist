\section*{Question Paper}

\subsection*{Section A: Multi-Concept (5 Questions)}
\begin{enumerate}[label=Q\arabic*.]
\item A bacterial plasmid carrying a gene for antibiotic resistance is transformed into \textit{Escherichia coli}. If the plasmid also contains a lacZ reporter gene, which of the following observations confirms successful transformation?  
(A) Growth on antibiotic medium + blue colonies on X-gal plates  
(B) Growth on antibiotic medium + white colonies on X-gal plates  
(C) No growth on antibiotic medium + blue colonies  
(D) Growth on antibiotic medium only, no color change

\item In humans, a mutation in mitochondrial DNA affects ATP synthase. Which physiological system shows earliest symptoms?  
(A) Nervous system due to high ATP demand  
(B) Digestive system due to enzyme secretion  
(C) Skeletal system due to bone remodeling  
(D) Immune system due to antibody production

\item A cross between two heterozygous plants (AaBb) produces offspring. If gene A controls chlorophyll synthesis and gene B controls resistance to fungal infection, what fraction of progeny will be chlorophyll-deficient and fungus-susceptible?  
(A) 1/16 (B) 3/16 (C) 4/16 (D) 9/16

\item In a population of \textit{Drosophila}, Hardy–Weinberg equilibrium is disturbed due to migration. Which biotechnological tool can be used to track allele frequency changes across generations?  
(A) PCR + Gel electrophoresis  
(B) ELISA  
(C) Western blot  
(D) Northern blot

\item A patient with pituitary tumor shows abnormal secretion of ACTH. Which downstream effect is most likely?  
(A) Hyperthyroidism due to excess TSH  
(B) Hypercortisolism due to adrenal stimulation  
(C) Hypoglycemia due to insulin suppression  
(D) Reduced melatonin secretion
\end{enumerate}

\subsection*{Section B: Data/Experimental (5 Questions)}
\begin{enumerate}[label=Q\arabic*.]
\setcounter{enumi}{5}
\item A DNA replication experiment uses radioactive thymidine. After one round of replication in \textit{E. coli}, autoradiography shows half the DNA molecules radioactive. Which model of replication does this support?  
(A) Conservative (B) Semi-conservative (C) Dispersive (D) Random

\item In an experiment, nerve impulses are recorded from frog sciatic nerve. When Na$^+$ channels are blocked by tetrodotoxin, the action potential graph shows:  
(A) No depolarization phase  
(B) Reduced repolarization  
(C) Increased hyperpolarization  
(D) Normal impulse conduction

\item A population growth curve of \textit{Paramecium} shows logistic growth. At which phase is the carrying capacity reached?  
(A) Lag phase (B) Exponential phase (C) Stationary phase (D) Decline phase

\item A restriction digestion experiment yields fragments of 4 kb, 2 kb, and 1 kb. Which technique best confirms fragment sizes?  
(A) SDS-PAGE (B) Agarose gel electrophoresis (C) ELISA (D) Western blot

\item A student measures oxygen evolution in isolated chloroplasts under red light. Rate of photosynthesis is lower compared to white light. Why?  
(A) Red light excites only Photosystem I  
(B) Red light excites only Photosystem II  
(C) White light excites both photosystems  
(D) Red light inhibits Calvin cycle
\end{enumerate}

\subsection*{Section C: Assertion-Reason / Matching (5 Questions)}
\begin{enumerate}[label=Q\arabic*.]
\setcounter{enumi}{10}
\item Assertion (A): DNA polymerase cannot initiate synthesis without a primer.  
Reason (R): RNA primers provide free 3'-OH ends for elongation.  
(A) Both A and R true, R correct explanation  
(B) Both A and R true, R not explanation  
(C) A true, R false  
(D) A false, R true

\item Assertion (A): In humans, ADH secretion increases during dehydration.  
Reason (R): ADH promotes water reabsorption in distal convoluted tubule.  
(A) Both A and R true, R correct explanation  
(B) Both A and R true, R not explanation  
(C) A true, R false  
(D) A false, R true

\item Match the following:  
1. PCR $\rightarrow$ (a) Amplification of DNA  
2. ELISA $\rightarrow$ (b) Detection of antigen-antibody interaction  
3. Western blot $\rightarrow$ (c) Protein identification  
4. Northern blot $\rightarrow$ (d) RNA detection  

Correct match:  
(A) 1-a, 2-b, 3-c, 4-d  
(B) 1-b, 2-a, 3-d, 4-c  
(C) 1-c, 2-d, 3-a, 4-b  
(D) 1-a, 2-c, 3-b, 4-d

\item Assertion (A): In population ecology, density-dependent factors regulate growth.  
Reason (R): Disease spreads faster in dense populations.  
(A) Both A and R true, R correct explanation  
(B) Both A and R true, R not explanation  
(C) A true, R false  
(D) A false, R true

\item Assertion (A): In lac operon, lactose acts as inducer.  
Reason (R): Lactose binds repressor, preventing it from attaching to operator.  
(A) Both A and R true, R correct explanation  
(B) Both A and R true, R not explanation  
(C) A true, R false  
(D) A false, R true
\end{enumerate}
