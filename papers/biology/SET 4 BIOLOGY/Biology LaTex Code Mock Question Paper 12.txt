\section*{Question Paper}

\subsection*{Section A: Multi-Concept (5 Questions)}
\begin{enumerate}[label=Q\arabic*.]
\item A transgenic \textit{Oryza sativa} plant expresses a gene for iron enrichment. Which NCERT concept does this illustrate?  
(A) Biofortification  
(B) Bt cotton for pest resistance  
(C) Somatic hybridization  
(D) Micropropagation

\item A mutation in the gene coding for beta-globin chain leads to sickle cell anemia. Which combined physiological effect is observed?  
(A) Distorted RBC shape + reduced oxygen transport  
(B) Enhanced immunity + anemia  
(C) Increased bone density + reduced calcium absorption  
(D) Excessive sweating + hyperthyroidism

\item In a monohybrid cross Aa × Aa, what fraction of progeny will be homozygous recessive according to Mendel’s law?  
(A) 1/4  
(B) 1/2  
(C) 3/4  
(D) 1/8

\item A patient with hyperthyroidism shows high thyroxine but low TSH. Which NCERT concept explains this?  
(A) Negative feedback suppression  
(B) Pituitary autonomy  
(C) Adrenal insufficiency  
(D) Diabetes mellitus

\item In a population of \textit{Drosophila}, allele frequency of A = 0.4 and a = 0.6. Under Hardy–Weinberg equilibrium, what is expected frequency of heterozygotes?  
(A) 0.24  
(B) 0.36  
(C) 0.48  
(D) 0.60
\end{enumerate}

Reason (R): Insulin promotes glucose uptake by cells.  
(A) Both A and R true, R correct explanation  
(B) Both A and R true, R not explanation  
(C) A true, R false  
(D) A false, R true

\item Match the following (NCERT based):  
1. PCR $\rightarrow$ (a) DNA amplification  
2. ELISA $\rightarrow$ (b) Antigen-antibody detection  
3. Western blot $\rightarrow$ (c) Protein identification  
4. DNA sequencing $\rightarrow$ (d) Determines nucleotide order

(A) 1-a, 2-b, 3-c, 4-d  
(B) 1-b, 2-a, 3-d, 4-c  
(C) 1-c, 2-d, 3-a, 4-b  
(D) 1-a, 2-c, 3-b, 4-d

(D) A false, R true
