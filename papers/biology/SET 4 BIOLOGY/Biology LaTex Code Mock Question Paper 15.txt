\section*{Question Paper}

\subsection*{Section A: Multi-Concept (5 Questions)}
\begin{enumerate}[label=Q\arabic*.]
\item A transgenic \textit{Oryza sativa} plant expresses a gene for Vitamin A biosynthesis. Which NCERT concept does this illustrate?  
(A) Golden Rice project for biofortification  
(B) Bt cotton for pest resistance  
(C) Somatic hybridization  
(D) Micropropagation

\item A mutation in the gene coding for chloride ion channels leads to cystic fibrosis. Which combined physiological effect is observed?  
(A) Thick mucus secretion + impaired gas exchange  
(B) Enhanced immunity + anemia  
(C) Increased bone density + reduced calcium absorption  
(D) Excessive sweating + hyperthyroidism

\item In a dihybrid cross AaBb × AaBb, what fraction of progeny will show both dominant traits according to Mendel’s law?  
(A) 9/16  
(B) 3/16  
(C) 4/16  
(D) 1/16

\item A patient with iodine deficiency shows low thyroxine but high TSH. Which NCERT concept explains this?  
(A) Goitre due to thyroid gland failure  
(B) Pituitary autonomy  
(C) Negative feedback suppression  
(D) Adrenal hyperplasia

\item In a population of \textit{Drosophila}, allele frequency of A = 0.6 and a = 0.4. Under Hardy–Weinberg equilibrium, what is expected frequency of heterozygotes?  
(A) 0.24  
(B) 0.36  
(C) 0.48  
(D) 0.16
\end{enumerate}

\end{enumerate}

(B) Both A and R true, R not explanation  
(C) A true, R false

\item Assertion (A): Insulin secretion increases after carbohydrate intake.  
Reason (R): Insulin promotes glucose uptake by cells.  
(A) Both A and R true, R correct explanation  
(B) Both A and R true, R not explanation  
(C) A true, R false  
(D) A false, R true

\item Match the following (NCERT based):  
1. PCR $\rightarrow$ (a) DNA amplification  
2. ELISA $\rightarrow$ (b) Antigen-antibody detection  
3. Western blot $\rightarrow$ (c) Protein identification  
4. Northern blot $\rightarrow$ (d) RNA detection  

Correct match:  
(A) 1-a, 2-b, 3-c, 4-d  
(B) 1-b, 2-a, 3-d, 4-c  
(C) 1-c, 2-d, 3-a, 4-b  
(D) 1-a, 2-c, 3-b, 4-d

(C) A true, R false  
(D) A false, R true
