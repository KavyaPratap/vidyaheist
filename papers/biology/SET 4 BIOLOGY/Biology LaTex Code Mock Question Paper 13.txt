\section*{Question Paper}

\subsection*{Section A: Multi-Concept (5 Questions)}
\begin{enumerate}[label=Q\arabic*.]
\item A transgenic \textit{Brassica} plant expresses a gene for pest resistance. Which NCERT concept does this illustrate?  
(A) Bt toxin expression  
(B) Biofortification  
(C) Somatic hybridization  
(D) Micropropagation

\item A mutation in the gene coding for insulin receptor leads to Type II diabetes. Which combined physiological effect is observed?  
(A) Hyperglycemia + reduced glucose uptake  
(B) Hypoglycemia + anemia  
(C) Enhanced immunity + obesity  
(D) Excessive sweating + hyperthyroidism

\item In a dihybrid cross AaBb × AaBb, what fraction of progeny will show one dominant and one recessive trait according to Mendel’s law?  
(A) 6/16  
(B) 4/16  
(C) 3/16  
(D) 9/16

\item A patient with adrenal gland hypersecretion shows high cortisol but low ACTH. Which NCERT concept explains this?  
(A) Negative feedback suppression  
(B) Pituitary autonomy  
(C) Thyroid gland failure  
(D) Adrenal insufficiency

\item In a population of \textit{Drosophila}, allele frequency of A = 0.3 and a = 0.7. Under Hardy–Weinberg equilibrium, what is expected frequency of heterozygotes?  
(A) 0.21  
(B) 0.42  
(C) 0.49  
(D) 0.09
\end{enumerate}

\subsection*{Section B: Data/Experimental (5 Questions)}

\item Photosynthetic rate in isolated chloroplasts is measured under blue light vs. red light. Which observation is correct?  
(A) Blue light supports higher rate than red  
(B) Red light supports higher rate than blue  
(C) Both equal  
(D) Neither supports photosynthesis
\end{enumerate}

Reason (R): Insulin promotes glucose uptake by cells.  
(A) Both A and R true, R correct explanation  
(B) Both A and R true, R not explanation  
(C) A true, R false  
(D) A false, R true

\item Match the following (NCERT based):  
1. PCR $\rightarrow$ (a) DNA amplification  
2. ELISA $\rightarrow$ (b) Antigen-antibody detection  
3. Western blot $\rightarrow$ (c) Protein identification  
4. DNA microarray $\rightarrow$ (d) Gene expression profiling  

Correct match:  
(A) 1-a, 2-b, 3-c, 4-d  
(B) 1-b, 2-a, 3-d, 4-c  
(C) 1-c, 2-d, 3-a, 4-b  
(D) 1-a, 2-c, 3-b, 4-d

(D) A false, R true
