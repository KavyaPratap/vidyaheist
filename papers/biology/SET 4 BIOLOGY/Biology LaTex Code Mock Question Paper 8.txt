\section*{Question Paper}

\subsection*{Section A: Multi-Concept (5 Questions)}
\begin{enumerate}[label=Q\arabic*.]
\item A transgenic \textit{Oryza sativa} plant expresses a bacterial gene for salt tolerance. Which physiological change confirms success?  
(A) Increased stomatal opening under salinity stress  
(B) Enhanced accumulation of compatible solutes like glycine betaine  
(C) Higher transpiration rate  
(D) Reduced photosynthetic efficiency

\item A mutation in voltage-gated Ca$^{2+}$ channels affects cardiac muscle function. Which combined physiological effect is observed?  
(A) Impaired contraction + arrhythmia  
(B) Enhanced neurotransmitter release + hyperthyroidism  
(C) Increased bone density + reduced calcium absorption  
(D) Excessive sweating + anemia

\item In a dihybrid cross AaBb × AaBb, if recombination frequency is 10\%, what fraction of progeny will be recombinant?  
(A) 10\%  
(B) 20\%  
(C) 40\%  
(D) 50\%

\item A patient with pituitary tumor shows high ACTH but low cortisol. Which endocrine dysfunction explains this?  
(A) Adrenal insufficiency  
(B) Pituitary autonomy  
(C) Negative feedback suppression  
(D) Thyroid gland failure

\item In a population of \textit{Drosophila}, allele frequency of A = 0.2 and a = 0.8. Under Hardy-Weinberg equilibrium, what is expected frequency of heterozygotes?  
(A) 0.16  
(B) 0.32  
(C) 0.48  
(D) 0.64
\end{enumerate}

\subsection*{Section B: Data/Experimental (5 Questions)}
\begin{enumerate}[label=Q\arabic*.]
\setcounter{enumi}{5}

(A) Conservative (B) Semi-conservative (C) Dispersive (D) Random

\item An experiment records action potentials in mammalian neurons. When extracellular K$^+$ concentration is increased, the graph shows:  
(A) Reduced resting potential magnitude  
(B) Increased depolarization amplitude  
(C) No change in repolarization  
(D) Enhanced hyperpolarization

\item A bacterial population shows logistic growth. At which phase is carrying capacity reached?  
(A) Lag phase  
(B) Exponential phase  
(C) Stationary phase  
(D) Decline phase

\item A restriction digestion of plasmid yields fragments of 6 kb and 4 kb. Which technique confirms fragment sizes?  
(A) SDS-PAGE  
(B) Agarose gel electrophoresis  
(C) ELISA  
(D) Western blot

\item Photosynthetic rate in isolated chloroplasts is measured under red light vs. green light. Which observation is correct?  
(A) Red light supports higher rate than green  
(B) Green light supports higher rate than red  
(C) Both equal  
(D) Neither supports photosynthesis
\end{enumerate}

Reason (R): Thyroxine stimulates mitochondrial activity.  
(A) Both A and R true, R correct explanation  
(B) Both A and R true, R not explanation  
(C) A true, R false  
(D) A false, R true

\item Match the following:  
1. DNA ligase $\rightarrow$ (a) Seals nicks in DNA backbone  
2. ELISA $\rightarrow$ (b) Antigen-antibody detection

(A) 1-a, 2-b, 3-c, 4-d  
(B) 1-b, 2-a, 3-d, 4-c  
(C) 1-c, 2-d, 3-a, 4-b  
(D) 1-a, 2-c, 3-b, 4-d

(D) A false, R true
