\section*{Question Paper}

\subsection*{Section A: Multi-Concept (5 Questions)}
\begin{enumerate}[label=Q\arabic*.]
\item A transgenic \textit{Brassica} plant expresses Bt toxin gene. Which physiological change confirms success?  
(A) Resistance to lepidopteran larvae feeding  
(B) Increased photosynthetic rate  
(C) Enhanced nitrogen fixation  
(D) Higher seed oil content

\item A mutation in voltage-gated Na$^+$ channels affects neuron function. Which combined physiological effect is observed?  
(A) Failure of depolarization + impaired impulse conduction  
(B) Excessive neurotransmitter release + hyperthyroidism  
(C) Enhanced immune response + anemia  
(D) Increased bone density + reduced calcium absorption

\item In a trihybrid cross AaBbCc × AaBbCc, what fraction of progeny will be heterozygous for all three traits?  
(A) 1/64  
(B) 27/64  
(C) 9/64  
(D) 36/64

\item A patient with pituitary adenoma shows high TSH but low thyroxine. Which endocrine dysfunction explains this?  
(A) Thyroid gland failure  
(B) Pituitary autonomy  
(C) Negative feedback suppression  
(D) Adrenal hyperplasia

\item In a population of \textit{Drosophila}, allele frequency of A = 0.8 and a = 0.2. Under Hardy–Weinberg equilibrium, what is expected frequency of heterozygotes?  
(A) 0.16  
(B) 0.32  
(C) 0.48  
(D) 0.64
\end{enumerate}

\subsection*{Section B: Data/Experimental (5 Questions)}
\begin{enumerate}[label=Q\arabic*.]
\setcounter{enumi}{5}
\item A Meselson–Stahl experiment shows DNA bands at intermediate density after one generation. Which replication model does this support?  
(A) Conservative (B) Semi-conservative (C) Dispersive (D) Random

\item An experiment records action potentials in mammalian neurons. When extracellular K$^+$ concentration is reduced, the graph shows:  
(A) Hyperpolarization of resting potential  
(B) Reduced depolarization amplitude  
(C) Increased repolarization rate  
(D) No change in impulse conduction

\item A bacterial population shows logistic growth. At which phase is carrying capacity reached?  
(A) Lag phase  
(B) Exponential phase  
(C) Stationary phase  
(D) Decline phase

\item A restriction digestion of plasmid yields fragments of 4 kb, 3 kb, and 2 kb. Which technique confirms fragment sizes?  
(A) SDS-PAGE  
(B) Agarose gel electrophoresis  
(C) ELISA  
(D) Western blot

\item Photosynthetic rate in isolated chloroplasts is measured under blue light vs. far-red light. Which observation is correct?  
(A) Blue light supports higher rate than far-red  
(B) Far-red supports higher rate than blue  
(C) Both equal  
(D) Neither supports photosynthesis
\end{enumerate}

\subsection*{Section C: Assertion-Reason / Matching (5 Questions)}
\begin{enumerate}[label=Q\arabic*.]
\setcounter{enumi}{10}
\item Assertion (A): DNA polymerase I removes RNA primers.

(A) Both A and R true, R correct explanation  
(B) Both A and R true, R not explanation  
(C) A true, R false  
(D) A false, R true

\item Assertion (A): Cortisol secretion increases during stress.  
Reason (R): Cortisol stimulates gluconeogenesis and suppresses immune response.  
(A) Both A and R true, R correct explanation  
(B) Both A and R true, R not explanation  
(C) A true, R false  
(D) A false, R true

\item Match the following:  
1. PCR $\rightarrow$ (a) DNA amplification  
2. ELISA $\rightarrow$ (b) Antigen-antibody detection  
3. Western blot $\rightarrow$ (c) Protein identification  
4. DNA microarray $\rightarrow$ (d) Gene expression profiling  

Correct match:  
(A) 1-a, 2-b, 3-c, 4-d  
(B) 1-b, 2-a, 3-d, 4-c  
(C) 1-c, 2-d, 3-a, 4-b  
(D) 1-a, 2-c, 3-b, 4-d

\item Assertion (A): Density-dependent factors regulate population growth.

(A) Both A and R true, R correct explanation  
(B) Both A and R true, R not explanation  
(C) A true, R false  
(D) A false, R true

\item Assertion (A): In lac operon, glucose presence suppresses lactose utilization.

(A) Both A and R true, R correct explanation  
(B) Both A and R true, R not explanation  
(C) A true, R false  
(D) A false, R true
