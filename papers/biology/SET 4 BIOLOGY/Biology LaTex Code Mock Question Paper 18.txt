\subsection*{Section A: Multi-Concept (5 Questions)}
\begin{enumerate}[label=Q\arabic*.]
\item A transgenic \textit{Oryza sativa} plant expresses genes for iron and vitamin A enrichment. Which NCERT concept does this illustrate?  
(A) Biofortification  
(B) Bt cotton for pest resistance  
(C) Somatic hybridization  
(D) Micropropagation

\item A mutation in the gene coding for insulin receptor leads to Type II diabetes. Which combined physiological effect is observed?  
(A) Hyperglycemia + reduced glucose uptake  
(B) Hypoglycemia + anemia  
(C) Enhanced immunity + obesity  
(D) Excessive sweating + hyperthyroidism

\item In a monohybrid cross Aa × Aa, what fraction of progeny will be homozygous recessive according to Mendel’s law?  
(A) 1/4  
(B) 1/2  
(C) 3/4  
(D) 1/8

\item A patient with thyroidectomy shows low thyroxine but high TSH. Which NCERT concept explains this?  
(A) Goitre due to thyroid gland failure  
(B) Pituitary autonomy  
(C) Negative feedback suppression  
(D) Adrenal insufficiency

\item In a population of \textit{Drosophila}, allele frequency of A = 0.3 and a = 0.7. Under Hardy–Weinberg equilibrium, what is expected frequency of heterozygotes?  
(A) 0.21  
(B) 0.42  
(C) 0.49  
(D) 0.09
\end{enumerate}

\subsection*{Section D: Matching (2 Questions)}
\begin{enumerate}[label=Q\arabic*.]
\setcounter{enumi}{13}
\item Match the following (NCERT based):  
1. PCR $\rightarrow$ (a) DNA amplification  
2. ELISA $\rightarrow$ (b) Antigen-antibody detection  
3. Western blot $\rightarrow$ (c) Protein identification  
4. Northern blot $\rightarrow$ (d) RNA detection  

Correct match:  
(A) 1-a, 2-b, 3-c, 4-d  
(B) 1-b, 2-a, 3-d, 4-c  
(C) 1-c, 2-d, 3-a, 4-b  
(D) 1-a, 2-c, 3-b, 4-d

\item Match the following hormones with functions:  
1. Insulin $\rightarrow$ (a) Lowers blood glucose  
2. Glucagon $\rightarrow$ (b) Raises blood glucose  
3. Thyroxine $\rightarrow$ (c) Increases basal metabolic rate  
4. ADH $\rightarrow$ (d) Promotes water reabsorption  

Correct match:  
(A) 1-a, 2-b, 3-c, 4-d  
(B) 1-b, 2-a, 3-d, 4-c  
(C) 1-c, 2-d, 3-a, 4-b  
(D) 1-a, 2-c, 3-b, 4-d
\end{enumerate}
