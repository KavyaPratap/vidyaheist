\section*{Question Paper}

\subsection*{Section A: Multi-Concept (5 Questions)}
\begin{enumerate}[label=Q\arabic*.]
\item A transgenic \textit{Zea mays} plant expresses a gene for drought tolerance and insect resistance simultaneously. Which physiological changes confirm success?  
(A) Increased stomatal opening + higher transpiration  
(B) Enhanced proline accumulation + resistance to lepidopteran larvae  
(C) Reduced photosynthetic efficiency + higher seed oil content  
(D) Increased nitrate uptake + thicker cuticle

\item A mutation in voltage-gated K$^+$ channels affects neuron function. Which combined physiological effect is observed?  
(A) Impaired repolarization + prolonged action potential  
(B) Enhanced neurotransmitter release + hyperthyroidism  
(C) Increased bone density + reduced calcium absorption  
(D) Excessive sweating + anemia

\item In a trihybrid cross AaBbCc × AaBbCc, what fraction of progeny will be homozygous dominant for all three traits?  
(A) 1/64  
(B) 27/64  
(C) 9/64  
(D) 1/8

\item A patient with hypothyroidism shows high TSH but low thyroxine. Which endocrine dysfunction explains this?  
(A) Thyroid gland failure  
(B) Pituitary autonomy  
(C) Negative feedback suppression  
(D) Adrenal hyperplasia

\item In a population of \textit{Drosophila}, allele frequency of A = 0.3 and a = 0.7. Under Hardy–Weinberg equilibrium, what is expected frequency of heterozygotes?  
(A) 0.21  
(B) 0.42  
(C) 0.49  
(D) 0.09
\end{enumerate}

\subsection*{Section B: Data/Experimental (5 Questions)}
\begin{enumerate}[label=Q\arabic*.]
\setcounter{enumi}{5}
\item A Meselson–Stahl experiment shows DNA bands at intermediate density after one generation. Which replication model does this support?  
(A) Conservative (B) Semi-conservative (C) Dispersive (D) Random

\item An experiment records action potentials in mammalian neurons. When extracellular Cl$^-$ concentration is increased, the graph shows:  
(A) Hyperpolarization of resting potential  
(B) Reduced depolarization amplitude  
(C) Increased repolarization rate  
(D) No change in impulse conduction

\item A bacterial population shows logistic growth. At which phase is carrying capacity reached?  
(A) Lag phase  
(B) Exponential phase  
(C) Stationary phase  
(D) Decline phase

\item A restriction digestion of plasmid yields fragments of 5 kb, 2 kb, and 1 kb. Which technique confirms fragment sizes?  
(A) SDS-PAGE  
(B) Agarose gel electrophoresis  
(C) ELISA  
(D) Western blot

\item Photosynthetic rate in isolated chloroplasts is measured under green light vs. blue light. Which observation is correct?  
(A) Blue light supports higher rate than green  
(B) Green light supports higher rate than blue  
(C) Both equal  
(D) Neither supports photosynthesis
\end{enumerate}

(A) Both A and R true, R correct explanation  
(B) Both A and R true, R not explanation  
(C) A true, R false  
(D) A false, R true
