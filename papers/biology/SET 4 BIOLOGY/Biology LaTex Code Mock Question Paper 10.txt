\section*{Question Paper}

\subsection*{Section A: Multi-Concept (5 Questions)}
\begin{enumerate}[label=Q\arabic*.]
\item A transgenic \textit{Brassica} plant expresses a gene for herbicide resistance. Which NCERT concept does this illustrate?  
(A) Biofortification  
(B) Genetic engineering for crop improvement  
(C) Somatic hybridization  
(D) Micropropagation

\item A mutation in the gene coding for insulin leads to diabetes mellitus. Which combined physiological effect is observed?  
(A) Hyperglycemia + glucosuria  
(B) Hypoglycemia + anemia  
(C) Enhanced immunity + obesity  
(D) Excessive sweating + hyperthyroidism

\item In a monohybrid cross Aa × Aa, what fraction of progeny will be heterozygous according to Mendel’s law?  
(A) 1/4  
(B) 1/2  
(C) 3/4  
(D) 1/8

\item A patient with adrenal insufficiency shows low cortisol but high ACTH. Which NCERT concept explains this?  
(A) Negative feedback failure  
(B) Pituitary autonomy  
(C) Thyroid gland failure  
(D) Adrenal hyperplasia

\item In a population of \textit{Drosophila}, allele frequency of A = 0.6 and a = 0.4. Under Hardy–Weinberg equilibrium, what is expected frequency of heterozygotes?  
(A) 0.24  
(B) 0.36  
(C) 0.48  
(D) 0.16
\end{enumerate}

\subsection*{Section B: Data/Experimental (5 Questions)}
\begin{enumerate}[label=Q\arabic*.]
\setcounter{enumi}{5}

(A) Conservative (B) Semi-conservative (C) Dispersive (D) Random

\item An experiment records action potentials in frog neurons. When extracellular K$^+$ concentration is increased, the graph shows:  
(A) Reduced resting potential magnitude  
(B) Increased depolarization amplitude  
(C) No change in repolarization  
(D) Enhanced hyperpolarization

\item A bacterial population shows logistic growth. At which phase is carrying capacity reached?  
(A) Lag phase  
(B) Exponential phase  
(C) Stationary phase  
(D) Decline phase

\item A restriction digestion of plasmid yields fragments of 4 kb and 2 kb. Which NCERT technique confirms fragment sizes?  
(A) SDS-PAGE  
(B) Agarose gel electrophoresis  
(C) ELISA  
(D) Western blot

\item Photosynthetic rate in isolated chloroplasts is measured under blue light vs. green light. Which observation is correct?  
(A) Blue light supports higher rate than green  
(B) Green light supports higher rate than blue  
(C) Both equal  
(D) Neither supports photosynthesis
\end{enumerate}

Reason (R): Insulin promotes glucose uptake by cells.  
(A) Both A and R true, R correct explanation  
(B) Both A and R true, R not explanation  
(C) A true, R false  
(D) A false, R true

\item Match the following (NCERT based):  
1. PCR $\rightarrow$ (a) DNA amplification  
2. ELISA $\rightarrow$ (b) Antigen-antibody detection  
3. Western blot $\rightarrow$ (c) Protein identification  
4. Northern blot $\rightarrow$ (d) RNA detection

(A) 1-a, 2-b, 3-c, 4-d  
(B) 1-b, 2-a, 3-d, 4-c  
(C) 1-c, 2-d, 3-a, 4-b  
(D) 1-a, 2-c, 3-b, 4-d
