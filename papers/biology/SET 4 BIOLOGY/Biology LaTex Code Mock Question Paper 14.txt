\section*{Question Paper}

\subsection*{Section A: Multi-Concept (5 Questions)}
\begin{enumerate}[label=Q\arabic*.]
\item A transgenic \textit{Solanum tuberosum} (potato) plant expresses a gene for delayed spoilage. Which NCERT concept does this illustrate?  
(A) Genetic engineering for crop improvement  
(B) Biofortification  
(C) Somatic hybridization  
(D) Micropropagation

\item A mutation in the gene coding for hemoglobin leads to thalassemia. Which combined physiological effect is observed?  
(A) Reduced hemoglobin synthesis + anemia  
(B) Enhanced immunity + anemia  
(C) Increased bone density + reduced calcium absorption  
(D) Excessive sweating + hyperthyroidism

\item In a trihybrid cross AaBbCc × AaBbCc, what fraction of progeny will be homozygous recessive for all three traits?  
(A) 1/64  
(B) 1/32  
(C) 1/16  
(D) 1/8

\item A patient with thyroidectomy (removal of thyroid gland) shows low thyroxine but high TSH. Which NCERT concept explains this?  
(A) Goitre due to thyroid gland failure  
(B) Pituitary autonomy  
(C) Negative feedback suppression  
(D) Adrenal insufficiency

\item In a population of \textit{Drosophila}, allele frequency of A = 0.2 and a = 0.8. Under Hardy–Weinberg equilibrium, what is expected frequency of heterozygotes?  
(A) 0.16  
(B) 0.32  
(C) 0.48  
(D) 0.64
\end{enumerate}

\subsection*{Section B: Data/Experimental (5 Questions)}
\begin{enumerate}[label=Q\arabic*.]
\setcounter{enumi}{5}
\item Meselson–Stahl experiment using N$^{15}$ DNA showed intermediate density bands after one generation. Which replication model does this support?  
(A) Conservative (B) Semi-conservative (C) Dispersive (D) Random

\item An experiment records action potentials in frog neurons. When extracellular Na$^+$ concentration is increased, the graph shows:  
(A) Increased depolarization amplitude  
(B) Reduced resting potential magnitude  
(C) No change in repolarization  
(D) Enhanced hyperpolarization

\item A bacterial population shows logistic growth. At which phase is carrying capacity reached?  
(A) Lag phase  
(B) Exponential phase  
(C) Stationary phase  
(D) Decline phase

\item A restriction digestion of plasmid yields fragments of 6 kb and 4 kb. Which NCERT technique confirms fragment sizes?  
(A) SDS-PAGE  
(B) Agarose gel electrophoresis  
(C) ELISA  
(D) Western blot

\item Photosynthetic rate in isolated chloroplasts is measured under blue light vs. green light. Which observation is correct?  
(A) Blue light supports higher rate than green  
(B) Green light supports higher rate than blue  
(C) Both equal  
(D) Neither supports photosynthesis
\end{enumerate}

\subsection*{Section C: Assertion-Reason / Matching (5 Questions)}
\begin{enumerate}[label=Q\arabic*.]
\setcounter{enumi}{10}
\item Assertion (A): DNA ligase joins Okazaki fragments.  
Reason (R): It seals nicks in sugar-phosphate backbone.  
(A) Both A and R true, R correct explanation  
(B) Both A and R true, R not explanation  
(C) A true, R false  
(D) A false, R true

\item Assertion (A): ADH secretion increases during dehydration.  
Reason (R): ADH promotes water reabsorption in collecting ducts.  
(A) Both A and R true, R correct explanation  
(B) Both A and R true, R not explanation  
(C) A true, R false  
(D) A false, R true

\item Match the following (NCERT based):  
1. PCR $\rightarrow$ (a) DNA amplification  
2. ELISA $\rightarrow$ (b) Antigen-antibody detection  
3. Western blot $\rightarrow$ (c) Protein identification  
4. DNA sequencing $\rightarrow$ (d) Determines nucleotide order  

Correct match:  
(A) 1-a, 2-b, 3-c, 4-d  
(B) 1-b, 2-a, 3-d, 4-c  
(C) 1-c, 2-d, 3-a, 4-b  
(D) 1-a, 2-c, 3-b, 4-d

\item Assertion (A): Density-dependent factors regulate population growth.  
Reason (R): Competition for resources intensifies with density.  
(A) Both A and R true, R correct explanation  
(B) Both A and R true, R not explanation  
(C) A true, R false  
(D) A false, R true

\item Assertion (A): In lac operon, lactose acts as inducer.  
Reason (R): Lactose binds repressor, preventing operator binding.  
(A) Both A and R true, R correct explanation  
(B) Both A and R true, R not explanation  
(C) A true, R false  
(D) A false, R true
\end{enumerate}
